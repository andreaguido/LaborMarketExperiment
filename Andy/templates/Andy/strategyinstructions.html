Explaining the Guess Task:

Description of the Task

For the Guess Task, you must provide your best guess about what wage the employer will offer you this period. Your payoff for the Guess Task is based on the accuracy of your guesses.

How to earn money for the Guess Task?

A guess is 'accurate' if it exactly matches the wage actually offered by the employer for this period. You will earn $5 if your guess is 'accurate'. Otherwise, you will earn nothing.


Example

Suppose for example that:
  • Your answer to the Guess Task is $4
  • If the employer actually offers a wage equal to $4, you earn $5
  • If your guess equals any other possible number (for example $3 or $10), then you earn nothing
